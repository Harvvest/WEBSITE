About Harvvest

Built by traders. Designed for students.

HARVVEST Stock Market Institute was born in August 2023 with a single belief — that every Indian deserves access to world-class financial education.

Founded by Abhishek Thakor and Bhawesh Khatri in Adajan, Surat, Harvvest was built from the ground up by people who have been in the market — not just studied it. From day one, our focus has been on practical, real-world learning that prepares students to handle real money with real confidence.

What started as a small offline institute in Surat has grown into Gujarat's most serious stock market education brand — with 500+ students trained, 4 courses running, and a community of learners growing every batch.

We are proud to be Surat's ONLY institute offering Portfolio Management training, the ONLY institute teaching Fundamental Analysis and Valuations, and the ONLY place in Surat where you can learn Trading, Investing, and Hedging — all under one roof.

At Harvvest, we don't just teach the market. We build market professionals.
